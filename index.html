Must-See Places in Indonesia

Hi there! This purpose of this website is to demonstrate the HTML, CSS and JavaScript that I've learned so far.

I am from the beautiful country of Indonesia. You've probably heard of Bali, but that's just one (very touristy) island on this archipelago of 17,000+ islands. Below is a random list of some places I've been in Indonesia. Click on each image to learn more about each place!

Lombok

Skip Bali and go to Lombok. The waters are cleaner, the views are just as amazing and the culture is more representative of the rest of Indonesia.
When you're here, make sure to eat:
  • ayam taliwang
  • sate rembiga

Padang

Padang is home to some of the best food you'll ever eat and some of the best sunsets you'll ever see.
When you're here, make sure to eat:
  • RENDANG!!!
  • sate padang
  • soto padang
  • nasi kapau

Yogyakarta

Yogyakarta, also known as Jogja, is a city with a lot of art and culture, as well as beautiful nature nearby.
When you're here, make sure to eat:
  • gudeg
  • bakpia

Gunung Andong

There are several mountains (some active volcanos!) in Central Java. This photo was taken at the peak of Gunung Andong. On a clear day, you can see the peaks of 7 other mountains from this peak! You'll see:
  • Merapi
  • Merbabu
  • Sindoro
  • Sumbing
  • Prau
  • Ungaran
  • Telomoyo
